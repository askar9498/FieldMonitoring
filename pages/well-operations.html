Well Cementing

Performed well cementing to isolate production zones and prevent crossflow between different reservoir zones. This process involved pumping cement into the well to seal the casing and improve well integrity.

Wellbore Cleanout

Executed wellbore cleanout to remove debris, scale, and any blockages that could impede the flow of oil and gas. This operation used high-pressure fluid and mechanical tools to clean the wellbore for future operations.

Production Testing

Performed production testing to evaluate the well's flow rate, pressure, and temperature under different conditions. This helped determine the well's productivity and optimize production parameters.

Well Logging

Conducted well logging to assess formation properties, porosity, and permeability. The data collected helped understand the geological structure and the potential for hydrocarbon production.

August 2016
Hydraulic Fracturing

Executed hydraulic fracturing (fracking) to stimulate production by creating fractures in the rock formation. This operation used high-pressure fluids to improve permeability and enhance the flow of hydrocarbons.

February 2015
Well Acidizing

Applied acidizing treatment to enhance permeability and clean the wellbore. This process used hydrochloric acid to dissolve mineral deposits and improve the flow of oil or gas from the reservoir to the wellbore.

November 2013
Well Intervention

Performed a well intervention to repair tubing leaks and other damage that affected the production efficiency. This operation required specialized equipment to access the well and fix the issues while minimizing downtime.
